Tiramisu Recipe

About

The classic Italian dessert! A creamy concoction, melts in your mouth.

Ingredients

  • 1 Package Savoiardi Ladyfingers
  • 250 Grams Mascarpone Cheese
  • Cocoa Powder
  • 3 egg
  • 100 Grams Sugar
  • Coffee (Espresso)

Steps

  1. Make 2 (6 cup) pots of espresso. This is best made with a Bialetti Stovetop Espresso Maker and Kimbo Napoletano Ground Espresso.
  2. Pour the espresso into a shallow bowl or baking dish and set aside to cool.
  3. Separate the egg yolks and whites into two bowls.
  4. Add half of the sugar to the yolks and beat until thick.
  5. Add the mascarpone and beat until smooth and creamy (add Marsala Wine to taste).
  6. Beat the egg whites with the other half of the sugar until stiff and glossy, like you would for a meringue.
  7. Fold the egg whites into the egg yolk mixture a little at a time, blending through with a spatula.
  8. Dip cookies one at a time in the coffee and layer into a rectangular baking dish.
  9. Spread a generous layer of the mascarpone mixture on top.
  10. Sprinkle with cocoa powder
  11. Repeat layers.
  12. Cover and refrigerate several hours or overnight before serving.
